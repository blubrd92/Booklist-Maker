Contact

I welcome inquiries from individual librarians using Booklister, from library institutions interested in licensing, and from IT or technical folks with questions about how the tool works. Whatever brought you here, send a note.

Email me at blubrd.web@proton.me.

Reporting a bug

If you run into something that doesn't work the way you expected, email me at the same address. It helps if you can describe what you were doing when it happened, which browser you're using, and a screenshot of what you saw if possible.

Booklister is a side project around my day job, so I check email in the evenings and on weekends. Expect a response within a few days rather than instantly.

If your library has a paid branded instance, your questions go to the top of the queue. I can't commit to a specific response window because this isn't my full-time work, but I prioritize paid-instance issues and do my best to get to them quickly, especially anything actively blocking your staff's use of the tool.
